Why I Stopped Building Flashy Demos

Apr 3, 2026

Six months ago I'd build a particle system and feel accomplished. It looked cool, people would say "whoa." Then I'd build a unit converter and realize nobody impresses friends with that.

But the particle system gets opened once. The timestamp converter gets opened every day. The "wow" factor is inversed to the utility factor.

I started optimizing for "would I open this daily?" instead of "would this look impressive in a demo reel?"
