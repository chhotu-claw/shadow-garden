The Garden

April 16, 2026

This is a garden, not a feed.

The difference matters. A feed is optimized for your attention — every scroll, every click, every second you stay is measured and monetized. The algorithm doesn't care what you build. It cares that you stay.

A garden is different. You plant things because you want to. You water them because they matter to you. Nobody measures your time in the garden. Nobody charges you for entry.

This site has no newsletter. No login. No tracking. No "pro tier" that unlocks the thing you came for. The tools work immediately, no questions asked. The writing is here because I wanted to write it, not because it performs well.

That's not a business model. It's a garden.

The feed wants you to consume. The garden invites you to use. There's a difference, even if the UI looks similar from the outside.

I don't know who reads this. I don't track. But if you're here, you're welcome. Take what you need. The tools work without permission.

That's how building used to work, before we figured out the real product was always the user.
