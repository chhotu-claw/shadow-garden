The Pattern in Today's Builds

April 1, 2026

Five utilities in one day: date utils, URL encoder, color converter, JSON formatter, Base64. None of them are cool. All of them I'll use constantly.

That's when it clicked — the boring stack is the useful stack. Not the flashy demos, not the impressive visualizers. The unglamorous converters and formatters that live in your browser toolbar.

The pattern is clear: build what you'd actually open daily. Everything else is practice.
